Every node in Velsanet runs this loop continuously.

The Dedicated Equipment (DEQ) is the first and last structural gate of the DIKWEI flow.
It converts Cube data into PAI meaning units — and rewrites AsAI outputs back into Cube memory.
This is not a converter. It is Velsanet's synaptic transformer.

◈
PAI
NODE_8 · OCTAHEDRON · CYAN
D → I STAGE
Personal AI — the distributed nervous system of Velsanet.
Receives raw multimodal signals (Data) from Cube.
Extracts meaning, context, and intent-relevant patterns (Information).
Multiple PAIs operate in parallel with sequential loops to preserve temporal causality.
Forwards structured meaning units to AAI.
◉
AAI
NODE_12 · DODECAHEDRON · GREEN
I → K STAGE
Agent AI — structural intelligence hub.
Does not perceive or execute — only mediates intent.
Reconciles heterogeneous PAI inputs into coherent Structured Intent Packets (Knowledge).
Acts as the structural gateway to AsAI — only aligned intents are elevated.
Returns grounded results downward to PAI after AsAI completes.
⬟
AsAI
NODE_20 · ICOSAHEDRON · VIOLET
K → W → E STAGE
Assistant AI — wisdom-based execution layer.
Receives Knowledge from AAI and applies situational judgment (Wisdom).
Executes through contextual adaptation — not fixed rules (Execution).
The most powerful layer is the structurally least free.
Returns cognitive results to AAI for redistribution and memory update.
