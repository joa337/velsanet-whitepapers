
WP21 — AI Safety by Architecture
Velsanet White Paper #21 · v1.0 · 2026-03-04
This paper defines the architectural principle that AI safety cannot be achieved through rules or policies alone — it must be embedded in the structure itself.
The Velsanet three-layer AI architecture (PAI → AAI → AsAI) is a structural safety guarantee. Direct access from an individual to AsAI is architecturally impossible — not prohibited by rule, but absent by design.

A rule can be broken. An architectural impossibility cannot.
