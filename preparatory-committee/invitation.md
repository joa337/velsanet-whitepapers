## 1. What We Are Preparing

The Velsanet Research Institute exists to verify — on the bench, not on paper — a network architecture called **Velsanet**, built for a 6G world where humans, AI, and robots coexist, and to offer that verification as an international joint-research platform and open test-bed.

This preparatory committee is the group that, ahead of formal establishment, reviews the structure together, supports the policy process, and forms the founding core of the future research team.

---

## 2. Operating Model

**Qualification Model** — As with the Bluetooth SIG, only equipment that passes conformance verification against reference equipment can be admitted to the network. Verification is operated on a paid basis; verification fees and qualification-based royalties make the infrastructure self-sustaining.

**Research Operation** — An MIT-style open research model, with its home in **Saemangeum, Republic of Korea**. It also carries the strategic meaning of a nation establishing a 6G verification regime early.
