# Velsanet Research Institute

A structural platform for the realization of topology-first, AI-native network architecture.

---

## Purpose

The Velsanet Research Institute is proposed as a physical and governance foundation for the deployment and validation of the Velsanet architecture.

It is not a think tank.
It is not an incubator.
It is a structural execution platform.

The Institute exists to:

- Validate topology-first network models
- Prototype optical-native infrastructure (MOCT)
- Deploy three-layer AI (PAI / AAI / AsAI)
- Demonstrate sovereign structural governance (Q2–Q7)
- Establish city-scale experimental environments

---

## Architectural Position

The Research Institute corresponds to the physical realization layer of the Velsanet framework.

White Papers define the structure.
The Institute implements it.

Structural domains include:

- Polyhedral role layers (T4, C6, O8, D12, I20)
- Parallel optical E2E deployment
- Identity-bound path formation
- Projection-based governance hierarchy
- Q7–Q4 operational mapping

---

## Governance Model

The Institute operates under structural principles:

- No centralized control
- Sovereignty encoded in topology
- Participation through contribution
- Expansion through structural admissibility

The Institute is aligned with:

- IMT-2030 / 6G framework discussions
- ITU-T Study Group 13 contributions
- Open academic and industrial collaboration

---

## Location

Saemangeum Special Zone, Republic of Korea  
Proposed as a large-scale experimental and deployment ground for next-generation intelligent networks.

---

## Participation

Participation is open to:

- Governments (sovereign layer construction)
- Enterprises (technology validation & deployment)
- Universities and research institutions (formal study & standardization)
- International organizations (structural coordination)

Participation is structural, not symbolic.

This directory contains:

- Vision documents

- Deployment concept materials
- Institutional structural models
