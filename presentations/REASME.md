# Velsanet Presentations

This directory contains official presentation materials related to Velsanet’s standardization activities.

The contents are organized by contribution tracks such as TTA ICT Strategy and ITU-T SG13.

These materials are prepared for external communication, including standardization meetings, technical briefings, and strategic alignment discussions.

## Structure

- TTA-ICT-Strategy-2027  
  Presentation materials for Korea TTA ICT Standardization Strategy (Ver.2027)

- ITU-T-SG13  
  Presentation materials for ITU-T SG13 contributions (planned)

## Note

These presentations are aligned with Velsanet white papers and represent structured proposals for 6G architecture, AI-native networking, and topology-based design.

They are not final standards, but evolving contributions toward global standardization.
