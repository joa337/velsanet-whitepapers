# 01
Velsanet Network White Paper
Network Architecture Overview
The founding manifesto. A 3D polyhedral distributed network where intelligence flows as intent, not data. Five polyhedra (T₄ C₆ O₈ D₁₂ I₂₀) form the complete grammar of network space.

# 02
Wired–Wireless Convergence
Wireless as Optical Entry Point
Wireless is not a network — it is an entry point. Every device maps to one optical core (8 channels). V-MCU manages handover through overlapped E2E. No re-routing, no packet loss on mobility.
WIRELESS · E2E ↓ PDF
# 03
Multi-Optical-Core Transceiver
MOCT — Fixed Photonic Substrate
Packet switching abandoned entirely. Fixed, non-pluggable photonic module: 100–1,000+ parallel cores, MEMS alignment, pre-attached 5m+ fibers. The physical hardware heart of Velsanet.
HARDWARE · OPTICAL ↓ PDF
# 04
Matrix Architecture
8-Layer Optical Switching Fabric
Semiconductor-inspired crossbar matrix. 8 physically isolated optical layers per face-pair. E2E path = physical activation, not routing. Parallel E2E max = 8 × N(N−1)/2. Zero queuing. Zero arbitration.
MATRIX · PARALLELISM ↓ PDF

# 20
Node Intelligence & Optical Parallelism
The Self-Aware Core Configuration Architecture
Defines how Velsanet nodes recognize themselves and their neighbors, giving rise to intelligence. Introduces the first formal node naming schema, distinguishes T4 rhizome from proximity-based mesh, and explains how node intelligence beginning at O8 (PAI) enables hypercube-conformant connectivity and AI-requested optical parallelism.
NODE INTELLIGENCE · OPTICAL PARALLELISM ↓ PDF
# 21
AI Safety by Architecture
Why Direct Access to AI Must Be Structurally Impossible
Safety cannot be achieved through rules alone — it must be embedded in the structure itself. The three-layer AI architecture (PAI → AAI → AsAI) is a structural safety chain: direct individual access to AsAI is architecturally impossible. A rule can be broken; an architectural invariant cannot.
AI SAFETY · STRUCTURAL INVARIANT ↓ PDF
# 22
H6-Based 6G RAN Architecture
Structure-Native Radio Access Without DU/CU
Defines how a 6G ultra-small cell base station connects to one face of the H6 hexahedral node via MOCT ribbon cable (5m+), establishing the wireless-to-optical convergence boundary as a physical surface, not a software interface. DU and CU are structurally absent. Mobility is managed by an O8-centered system: Identity tracking, Overlapped E2E handover, and network-side signal selection.
6G RAN · MOBILITY · DU/CU-FREE ↓ PDF
# 23
6G RU: Multi-User Physical Mapping
Structure-Native Physical Path Direct Mapping (No-Switching)
Defines the hardware design specification of the Velsanet 6G RU — a structure-native wireless-to-optical transducer that maps multiple user signals directly to physical optical cores without software scheduling, switching, or buffering. Key components: 8-channel spatial multiplexing and Topology-Coupled Antenna (TCA).
6G RU · PHYSICAL MAPPING · NO-SWITCHING
